Kyōjurō Rengoku

The Flame Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps

Timeline of Kyōjurō Rengoku's Life

  • Early Training: Begins rigorous training in swordsmanship and combat techniques under the guidance of his father, Senjuro Rengoku, a former Flame Hashira.
  • Adolescence: Achieves remarkable proficiency in the Flame Breathing technique, mastering its various forms and developing his signature move, the Flame Tiger.
  • Demon Slayer Corps: Joins the Demon Slayer Corps at a young age, quickly rising through the ranks due to his exceptional skills and dedication to protecting humanity.
  • Demon Encounters: Engages in numerous battles against powerful demons, earning a reputation for his fearless nature and unwavering resolve in the face of danger.
  • Hashira Promotion: Eventually promoted to the rank of Flame Hashira, becoming one of the highest-ranking Demon Slayers within the organization.
  • Mugen Train Incident: Leads a crucial mission aboard the Mugen Train to eliminate a dangerous demon threat terrorizing passengers, demonstrating his unparalleled combat prowess and leadership abilities.
  • Legacy: Leaves behind a lasting legacy of bravery and selflessness, inspiring future generations of Demon Slayers to continue the fight against demons and protect humanity.
